Hi, I'm Nani Prasad

Educator & Tech Enthusiast

With over 5 years of experience in the technology and education field, I'm passionate about making complex technical concepts accessible to everyone. My journey began with a degree in Computer Science, followed by various roles in software development and data analysis.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with diverse teams and projects, from developing enterprise software to implementing data-driven solutions for businesses. This experience has given me a unique perspective on the practical applications of technology in real-world scenarios.

My teaching philosophy centers around practical, hands-on learning. I believe that the best way to master a skill is by doing, which is why all my courses and tutorials are designed with real-world projects and examples.
